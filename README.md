SmartAttend is a campus-focused system designed to bring fairness, transparency, and efficiency to attendance tracking and mess management in colleges.

In most institutions, students pay a fixed mess fee irrespective of whether they consume all meals. Due to classes, labs, internships, or personal reasons, students often miss meals but still pay the full amount. SmartAttend addresses this gap by introducing a usage-based model where missed meals are compensated using digital attendance coins.

Instead of penalizing students, SmartAttend rewards fairness.
